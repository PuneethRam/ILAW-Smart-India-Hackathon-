      SUITS RELATING TO MOVABLE PROPERTY

S.No. Description of suits Period of limitation Time from which period begins to run
1. For specific movable property lost, or acquired by theft, or dishonest misappropriation or conversion. Three years When the person hav­ing the right to the possession of the prop­erty first learns in whose possession it is.
2. For other specific movable property. Three years When the property is wrongfully taken.
3. To recover movable property deposited or pawned from a depository or pawnee Three years The date of refusal after demand.
4. To recover movable property deposited or pawned, and afterwards bought from the depository or pawnee for a valuable ;consideration. Three years When the sale becomes known to the plaintiff.
